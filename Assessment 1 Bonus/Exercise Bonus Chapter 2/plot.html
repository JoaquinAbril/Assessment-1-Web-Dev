Plot Overview

"Loki" follows the story of the God of Mischief after the events of "Avengers: Endgame." Loki, played by Tom Hiddleston, finds himself facing the Time Variance Authority (TVA) after he escapes with the Tesseract during the Avengers' time heist.

He is enlisted by the TVA to fix the timeline he broke by meddling with time, leading to a series of adventures across time and space.
